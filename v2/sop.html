Vision & guiding principles

Why the ROS exists and the rules it runs by.

Each i3 system performs its own function well, but they operate as independent applications that require employees to manually connect information and decide the next action. ROS is the AI layer that gathers information, recommends actions, automates repetitive work, identifies risk, and learns from every interaction — without replacing existing investments.

The vision

  • Every employee begins each day knowing exactly what to do next.
  • Every customer interaction improves organizational intelligence.
  • Every proposal reflects i3 best practices.
  • Every manager coaches from predictive insights instead of historical reports.
  • Every executive has real-time visibility into revenue health.

Guiding principles

1
People first. AI augments human decision-making.
2
One source of truth. Enter once, share securely.
3
AI before manual. Automate first; require human approval where judgment is needed.
4
Standardized execution. Every team follows the same workflow.
5
Continuous learning. Every opportunity improves the platform.
6
Privacy & compliance by design.
7
Business-outcome focused.

The 7-stage i3 sales cycle

From the i3 Sales Process (2021). The operational backbone the SOPs standardize.

Sales pipeline (prospect → close)

Most pipelines run four stages: Prospecting → Meeting → Proposal → Closing. Nurture it (set deadlines, always reach the decision-maker), and audit it regularly — remove unqualified leads and reinforce the trends that close (financing, company size, budget). In the i3 SSP program the anchor or a seasoned BDM joins at Stage 4; done right, 70–90% of solid opportunities close.
